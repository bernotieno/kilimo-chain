1. John Deere

John Deere is a leading manufacturer of agricultural machinery and equipment.

Founded in 1837, John Deere is an American corporation that manufactures agricultural, construction, and forestry machinery, diesel engines, drivetrains used in heavy equipment, and lawn care equipment. The company is known for its iconic green and yellow tractors and combines, which are widely used by farmers around the world. John Deere’s commitment to innovation and sustainability has made it a trusted name in the agriculture industry

2. Syngenta

Syngenta is a global agribusiness that produces agrochemicals and seeds

Syngenta, headquartered in Switzerland, is one of the world’s largest agricultural companies, specializing in crop protection and seed products. The company was formed in 2000 by the merger of Novartis Agribusiness and Zeneca Agrochemicals. Syngenta’s products help farmers increase crop yields and protect crops from pests, diseases, and weeds. The company is also involved in research and development to create sustainable agricultural solutions

3. Bayer Crop Science

Bayer Crop Science is a division of Bayer AG focused on agricultural products.

Bayer Crop Science, a division of the German multinational Bayer AG, is dedicated to providing innovative solutions for agriculture. The company offers a wide range of products, including seeds, crop protection chemicals, and digital farming tools. Bayer Crop Science aims to support farmers in producing high-quality food, feed, and fiber while minimizing environmental impact. The company’s research and development efforts focus on improving crop yields and sustainability.

4. AGCO Corporation

AGCO Corporation is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and distribution of agricultural equipment.

AGCO Corporation, headquartered in Duluth, Georgia, USA, is a major manufacturer and distributor of agricultural equipment and solutions. Founded in 1990, AGCO offers a comprehensive range of products, including tractors, combines, hay tools, sprayers, and forage equipment. The company’s brands, such as Massey Ferguson, Fendt, and Valtra, are well-known in the farming community. AGCO is committed to providing high-tech solutions to improve farm productivity and efficiency.

5. Corteva Agriscience

Corteva Agriscience is a major American agricultural chemical and seed company.

Corteva Agriscience, spun off from DowDuPont in 2019, is a leading pure-play agriculture company. Headquartered in Wilmington, Delaware, USA, Corteva provides farmers with a wide range of seed and crop protection products. The company’s mission is to enrich the lives of those who produce and those who consume, ensuring progress for generations to come. Corteva’s innovative solutions help farmers maximize productivity and sustainability.
